# Parent Privacy Boundary

This rule is product-critical and cannot move.

Parents may see:

- Subscription status and billing.
- A simple engagement signal, such as "active 4 times this week."
- Public browse answers.
- Parent-only assets and newsletters.

Parents may not see:

- The player's questions.
- The player's answers.
- The player's profile.
- The player's reflections.
- The player's saved items.

For players under 13, implement explicit parental consent before account use.
For players 13 to 17, the locker room is private from the parent by default.

Do not add a parent dashboard feature that exposes student content.
